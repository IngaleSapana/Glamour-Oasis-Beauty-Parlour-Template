What are your hours of operation?

We are open Monday to Saturday from 9:00 AM to 8:00 PM. On Sundays, we are open from 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM.

Do I need to make an appointment?

While walk-ins are welcome, we highly recommend making an appointment to ensure you receive the service you desire without waiting.

How can I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment by calling us at 9181716253, through our website’s booking system, or by visiting our salon in person.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, credit/debit cards, and mobile payment options like Paytm and Google Pay.

What is your cancellation policy?

We kindly ask that you provide at least 24 hours' notice if you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment. Cancellations made within 24 hours may be subject to a cancellation fee.
